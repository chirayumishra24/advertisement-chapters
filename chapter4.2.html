Advertising for Social Good

In today’s media landscape, advertising has evolved beyond selling products to advocating for social change and inclusivity.

This shift ensures that messages reach every corner of society — including marginalized groups and the differently-abled (Divyangjan).

1. Nukkad Nataks: The Original Viral Advertising

Street plays are one of India’s oldest and most effective “advertising” tools for social causes.

Unlike a TV ad you can skip, a Nukkad Natak happens right in your space — marketplaces, street corners, parks — impossible to ignore.

Key Techniques Used:

  • High Energy & Catchy Jingles: Loud rhythmic chanting + drums = instant attention (like a brand sonic logo)
  • Satire and Humor: Laugh at bad habits → sting of realization
  • Call to Action (CTA): Ends with direct appeal (“Keep your street clean”, “Take pride in your monument”)
  • Interactive Engagement: Actors break the fourth wall → turn viewers into participants

2. Inclusive Design: Advertising for All

Inclusive design creates ads accessible to everyone — regardless of physical or cognitive abilities.

For Divyangjan, this means going beyond visual/auditory-only content.

A. Braille in Advertising

For the visually impaired, traditional billboards are invisible. Inclusive brands are experimenting with:

  • Tactile Posters: Raised textures + Braille text → message “read” by touch
  • Product Packaging: Brands like Kellogg’s & Mastercard use touch cards / high-contrast codes

B. Sign Language & Audio Features

  • Sign Language Interpreters: Picture-in-Picture window with professional signer
  • Closed Captioning (CC): Essential text overlays for deaf community
  • Audio Description: Secondary audio track describes visuals during dialogue gaps

Examples of Inclusive Elements

Feature Target Group Benefit
Braille/Tactile Visually Impaired Enables reading through touch
Sign Language Hearing Impaired Provides natural language communication
High Contrast Low Vision/Color Blind Makes text and shapes easier to distinguish
Voice Commands Dexterity Issues Allows hands-free interaction with ads

Why This Matters

When advertising embraces social good and inclusivity, it stops being just a commercial tool.

It becomes a force for community building — ensuring “pride in heritage” or “cleanliness drives” are shared responsibilities for every citizen.
