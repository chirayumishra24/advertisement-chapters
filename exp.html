Step-by-Step Guide (Use the Tool Above)

1. Write Your Logo Prompt

Describe your brand in detail:

  • Brand name (e.g., "Thar Wanderer")
  • Style (minimalist / modern / vintage)
  • Colors (orange, gold, blue…)
  • Elements (camel, dunes, sun…)
  • Mood (adventurous, elegant…)
Example:

"Minimalist logo for Rajasthan desert safari brand 'Thar Wanderer'. Warm orange, gold, deep blue. Simple camel and sand dunes. Clean, adventurous, modern style."

2. Generate & Refine

Enter prompt → Generate → Review options → Edit prompt & retry → Choose favorite.

3. Download

Download high-res version (PNG/SVG). Name it e.g. Chirayu_Logo.png

4. Submit to LMS

In your Learning Management System:

  • Go to the logo submission assignment
  • Upload your file
  • Add short note (optional): Inspiration? How it shows good logo principles?
  • Submit

Teacher will check creativity, simplicity, memorability & brand fit!

Enjoy creating your brand identity! 🎨✨
