SUSTAINABLE LOCAL BRAND KIT

Build a complete brand identity for a local eco-friendly business: Logo • Tagline • Jingle • 30s Ad Script • Pitch like a pro!

YOUR MISSION – CREATE A HERO BRAND!

Develop a full kit for a sustainable local brand:
Logo • 3-7 word tagline • Jingle • 30-second ad script
Present with moodboard, style guide, and video demo!

STEP 1: CHOOSE YOUR BRAND IDEA

Your brand must be:

LOCAL
ECO-FRIENDLY / SUSTAINABLE
SOLVES A REAL PROBLEM

STEP 2: CREATE THE LOGO

Use Canva or any tool → Submit link on Skillizee Portal

Deliverables:

  • Digital / sketched logo
  • Full color version
  • Black & white OR icon-only version

Think about:

  • Simple shapes
  • Nature symbols (leaf, water, sun, earth)
  • Scales well even at small size

STEP 3: WRITE A TAGLINE (3–7 WORDS)

Make it EMOTIONAL — not just descriptive!

Avoid:

“Eco Friendly Products”

“Sustainable Local Goods”

Great examples:

“Small Choices. Big Earth Impact.”

“Good for You. Better for Earth.”

🎯 Focus: Memorable + Benefit-focused

STEP 4: CREATE A JINGLE (30 SECONDS)

Record & upload video on Skillizee Portal

Simple Structure Idea:

  1. Problem line
  2. Brand solution
  3. Feel-good ending with brand name

Example:
🎶 “Plastic waste everywhere we go…
Time to choose a better way to grow…
[Your Brand] makes it easy every day…
Love your planet — switch today!” 🎶

You can:

  • Sing live
  • Record on phone
  • Add claps / desk beats

🎯 Evaluation: Catchy rhythm + Recall power
