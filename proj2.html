YOUR MISSION – CREATE A HERO AD!

Write & perform a short 15–30 second video-style ad.
Then pitch it like a pro to your “client” (class/teacher) with moodboard + style guide!

BASIC FORMAT (TIMING GUIDE)

0–5 sec
SHOW THE PROBLEM
5–15 sec
INTRODUCE PRODUCT
15–25 sec
SHOW BENEFITS
Last 5 sec
TAGLINE + BRAND NAME

Example Flow:
👩 “Why do we throw so much plastic every day?”
👦 “What if we could reuse instead?”
🎥 Show product
🎙 “Introducing ___ — the smart eco choice.”
🌍 “Good for you. Better for Earth.”

🎯 Evaluation Focus: Message clarity + Engagement

PREPARE YOUR CLIENT PITCH

PRESENTATION

You are presenting to a “client” (teacher/class). Make it 2–3 minutes and wow them!

1. MOODBOARD

A page showing your brand’s visual vibe:

  • Colors
  • Nature images
  • Textures (jute, leaves, water, earth)
  • Words that describe the feeling (fresh, pure, hopeful, clean)

2. STYLE GUIDE (Mini Version)

Explain your brand rules:

  • Brand colors (2–3 max)
  • Font style (bold, handwritten, modern, etc.)
  • Tone of voice (friendly, inspiring, earthy)

3. VIDEO DEMO

Act out your ad OR show a recorded version.

Bring energy — make them feel your brand comes alive!

WHAT TO SUBMIT

Element Deliverable
Logo Drawing/digital + variations
Tagline 3–7 word emotional promise
Jingle 30s audio or live performance
Ad Script + acted or recorded demo
Moodboard Visual theme sheet
Style Guide Colors, fonts, tone
Pitch 2–3 min presentation

PRESENTATION STRUCTURE (2–3 Minutes)

  1. Introduce brand & problem
  2. Show logo + explain meaning
  3. Say tagline
  4. Perform/play jingle
  5. Act or show ad
  6. Explain why your brand is good for the planet
