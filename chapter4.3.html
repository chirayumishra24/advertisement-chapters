Key Roles & Their Contributions
  • Graphic Designers
    The "Visual Storytellers." Turn abstract ideas into images, logos, layouts — goal: instant recognition & aesthetic pleasure.
  • Content Writers
    The "Voice of the Brand." Craft headlines, captions, scripts — masters of persuasive language.
  • Public Relations (PR) Managers
    The "Reputation Guardians." Manage brand/cause image — ensure messages reach news outlets & build public trust.
  • Social Media Strategists
    The "Digital Architects." Decide platforms, timing, analyze engagement data.

Medium for Making the Advertisement

Whatever medium you choose, core elements (logo, tagline, CTA) stay the same — only presentation changes.

Poster Advertisements

  1. What will be the title of the poster? (grab attention instantly)
  2. What visual elements will support the message?
  3. If using photographs — source & attribution?
  4. How will you establish emotional connect?
  5. Colours & design elements for eye-catching appeal?

Considering the above, sketch the poster layout in the space below (or describe it).

Digital / Animated Advertisements

Replicate your poster using tools like PowerPoint or Canva.
What differences did you notice between hand-made and digital creation?

New Elements for Digital/Animated Ads

  1. Music & sound — background music / sound effects?
  2. Scriptwriting — dialogue, voiceover, narration?
  3. Storyboard — key frames & actions/text?
  4. Design & animation elements you will use?
  5. Type of animation (fade in, zoom, slide)?
  6. Will animation create emotional connect? How?

Review Before Sharing

  • Creativity: Unique & engaging?
  • Clarity of message: Tagline & CTA easy to understand? Benefits clear?
  • Visual appeal: Attractive & theme-aligned?
