1. Establishing the Feedback Loop

Feedback isn’t critique — it’s conversation.

Owner + Expert = two different filters that turn your draft into legacy material:

  • Business Owner: “Does this feel like us? Will customers recognize it?”
  • Industry Expert: “Is typography legible? Resolution correct? Trend-aligned?”

The Three-Point Check

  1. Clarity – obvious in 3 seconds?
  2. Creativity – thumb-stopping moment?
  3. Visual Appeal – balanced or cluttered?

2. Real-World Application: The "Buy-In"

The make-or-break question:
“Would you actually use this for your business?”

One answer separates classroom creativity from commercial legacy.

If “No”

This is gold — not failure.

Ask: “What specifically holds it back?”

If “Yes”

You crossed the bridge.

Student → consultant pipeline complete.
